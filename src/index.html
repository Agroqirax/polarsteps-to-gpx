Polarsteps to GPX Converter

Seamlessly convert your Polarsteps travel data into GPX tracks.

Export your Polarsteps data as a ZIP file, then upload it below to generate your GPX tracks.
  • Private & Secure: All processing happens locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
  • Quick & Easy: Conversion takes just a few seconds, even for large trips.
  • Open Source: See GitHub for instructions, source code, and license.
Only ZIP files exported from Polarsteps are supported.
